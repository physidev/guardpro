Red/yellow/green band rules

Level Definition Pool Access
Green

Those who pass green swim test.

  • Swim 25 yards front crawl with rhythmic breathing and overarm recovery
  • Jump into pool from side, tread water for 1 minute, no floating
  • Access to whole pool

7 and under:

  • Must have a parent/guardian in the water within arm's reach
Yellow

Those who pass yellow swim test.

  • Swim to safety line that divides shallow end and deep end
  • Float on back or tread for 30 seconds
  • Jump into pool from side and return to edge
  • Access to shallow pool

7 and under:

  • Must have a parent/guardian in the water within arm's reach

8 and above:

  • If required floatation to pass the test, must have a parent/guardian within arm's reach
Red

Individuals who do not want to take a swim test or those who do not meet the yellow or green band requirements

7 and under:

  • Access to shallow water, wearing a life jacket
  • Must have a parent/guardian in the water within arm's reach

8 and above:

  • Access to shallow end up to their armpits while standing flat footed
  • A life jacket is required for water above armpits
  • Must have a parent/guardian within arm's reach
